About Me!

Hi there! My name is Oliwia Dawid and I'm a Creative Digital Media student studying at TU Dublin Blanchardstown Campus.

I choose this course because I was interested in the modules and thought that they would be fun to do. Guess what? They are fun! Well for me at least. It may not be easy sometimes but I live this course and I try my best!

One of my hobbies is Art. I have been doing it for years now. I even did it in Secondary School for my Junior and Leaving Cert. Whenever I have time I sometime draw my characters, my friends or some random things, and other times I do Sudoku, Word searches or watch YouTube or Anime.

In the future I hope to to be an Illustrator or an Animater or a Graphic design so that I create works for people and not just myself.
